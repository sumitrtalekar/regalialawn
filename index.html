Welcome to Regalia Lawn

Regalia Lawn is a premier wedding venue in Nagpur, known as the best wedding lawn in Nagpur. Our spacious lawn accommodates over 1200 guests, offering a serene and elegant setting for weddings and events. With ample parking, open/closed catering options, and an in-house decoration team, we provide the flexibility to customize your celebration. The venue also features three luxurious rooms for the bride, groom, and family. Our lush green lawn is maintained to perfection, ensuring a stunning ambiance for your special day. Celebrate your unforgettable moments at Regalia Lawn.

Our Rooms

We offer three luxurious rooms to accommodate the bride, groom, and family. Perfect for resting, preparing, or just relaxing before and after the celebration.

Bride's Suite

A spacious, elegant, and private retreat perfect for the bride and her entourage to relax, get ready, and enjoy each other's company in comfort. Features include:

  • Large vanity area with lighted mirrors
  • Spacious seating area for the bridal party
  • Luxurious en-suite bathroom
  • Complimentary champagne on arrival

Groom's Den

A sophisticated and convenient space for the groom and his family members to unwind, get ready, and celebrate in style. Features include:

  • Premium entertainment system
  • Comfortable lounge area
  • Mini bar with premium beverages
  • Spacious wardrobe and dressing area

Family Oasis

A premium guest room for families who want to stay together and enjoy the celebration in comfort, with ample space for relaxation and bonding. Features include:

  • Connecting rooms available
  • Comfortable seating area
  • Extra beds for children
  • Complimentary breakfast
